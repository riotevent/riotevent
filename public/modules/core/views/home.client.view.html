Share events

With Riotevent you can easily create an event and start to share the link all around. You can publish any kind of event, and your are free on what you want to show on the event page. Any kind of event is welcome.

Take part

Riotevent wants to put in contact people who want to take part in events. Through a smart discussion system you can discuss about the event, find solutions, give propositions, ask organisers, ...

Move your ass

Riotevent is not an other webiste that will make you spend more time in front of your screen. Riotevent want to help people to share, find, and live events in real life.
